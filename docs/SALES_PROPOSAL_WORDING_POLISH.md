# Sales proposal wording polish

This release tightens the customer-facing commercial proposal wording so the PDF and Markdown exports sound like a clean sales proposal rather than a pilot task list.

## What changed

Customer-facing proposal exports now use stronger commercial wording:

- suggested next step says: "Book a 30-minute call to review the pilot outcome, confirm rollout scope, and agree the recurring VendorVerdict package."
- exports avoid wording such as "resolve the remaining pilot actions"
- success criteria avoid overly vendor-specific lines such as "why Notion was recommended most often"
- customer exports use a generic rollout phrase: "confirm the recommended vendor approach, remaining evidence gaps, and rollout priorities"

## Existing proposals

The proposal dashboard can keep the original internal text in the database. The customer PDF and Markdown export apply the safer customer-facing wording at export time, so older proposal records are also protected from weak sales wording.
