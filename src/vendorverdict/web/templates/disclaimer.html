VendorVerdict disclaimer

VendorVerdict helps teams structure SaaS procurement decisions. It is not a replacement for legal, security, privacy, financial, or procurement sign-off.

Procurement guidance only

Reports are decision-support artifacts based on the request, the scoring rubric, configured evidence, and public or fallback source checks. A human should review the output before making any buying decision.

Not a security audit

VendorVerdict does not conduct penetration testing, code review, vendor due-diligence interviews, contract review, or formal security certification.

Not legal advice

Reports may mention privacy, GDPR, security, or contractual topics, but they are not legal advice. Consult qualified professionals where legal, compliance, or regulated decisions are involved.

Evidence limitations

Public vendor pages can change, and fallback source targets may be incomplete. Use the generated due-diligence questions to verify critical claims directly with the vendor.

Pilot use

The pilot is intended to validate whether VendorVerdict fits your workflow, risk appetite, and decision process before broader use.
